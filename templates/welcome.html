Here is a run down of everything you need to know to get involved in LCC Tennis Club.

Booking A Court

Courts can be booked  here, or by going to the booking section in SportyHQ.

Courts 3, 4, 5, and 6 are bookable at all times. Courts 1 and 2 are available for walk-on use at all times. At peak times,  play is limited to 1 hour for singles and 1.5 hours for doubles games.

 

Thursday Social Tennis

We have a number of volunteers who run an excellent Social Tennis Night on Thursdays at 7.30pm. We feel this is a great way to meet and reacquaint with other club members. It is well organized in terms of matching the various skill levels, so you will be sure to get some good games.

Social Tennis Morning

Thursday mornings at 10.45am brings lots of ladies of leisure to the courts. Play lasts an hour approx. followed by coffee and chat. This is very relaxed, social tennis. All welcome. Just show up with a racket.
 

Club News

Information on upcoming events, court bookings, club successes, league, mini tournaments etc will be visible on your dashboard on SportyHQ. Newsletters will also be sent via email every month or so with all the latest updates.
 

Member Contact Details

All members are automatically added to SportyHQ. As well as for box leagues and ladders, this system is also a handy way for club members to contact each other. Please can all members keep their contact details up to date on this system.                                         
 

Our Singles Ladder

Check it out by going to SportyHQ. This is a great way to get some competitive tennis, so get challenging.
 

Tennis Club Rules

LCC Tennis should be fun for everyone. Please pay attention to the following:

  • When people are queuing for courts, play is limited to 1 hour for singles and 1.5 hours for doubles games.
  • Visitors may play with a member a maximum once a month. A fee of 5 must be paid and the visitor's name must be recorded in our Visitor Log book, in the bar.
 

Our Fitness Room

LCCs getting even better! There's a qualified personal trainer in LCC who does individual and group fitness training sessions and all members can avail of a discount for his initial consulting service!
 

Useful Information

Court light tokens cost 1 (discounted price) for 30 minutes and are available in the Club bar. These tokens can also be used for the changing room saunas. Tennis balls are also available for purchase at the bar.

Your membership card can be topped up and used to purchase items at the bar for a discount.
