Facilities

LCC offers access to five different sports and a fabulous complex with facilities a bar, a fitness room, function rooms, saunas, and modern comfortable changing rooms.

The LCC bar is run by Ronnie. It is serviced by a full time barmen. Our bar opens each day from 5pm until closing, and often earlier at the weekend if there is something on.

A fitness room is available, comprising mainly aerobic equipment, allowing you to stay fit when you're not actually playing tennis (although you should be playing).

There is also a function room, and an indoor hall that can both be booked by members to lay on events for the individual sections.
