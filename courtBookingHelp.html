Overview

  1. Courts 3/5/6 only are available for advance booking by all LCC members Mon-Fri from 5:30pm. All other courts will remain for walk-on use.
  2. Courts 3/5/6 can be booked up to 7 days in advance.
  3. Courts booked online do not require the use of the court booking board (located under the metal stariwell)
  4. At least two players must be on court 5 mins after the booking start time. Otherwise, the court becomes available for walk-on use by any member waiting for a court.
  5. Club captains can book any court at any time for the purposes of league matches, league practice etc, so please check the online court schedule arranging to play.
  6. Between Mon-Fri from 5:30pm, please only use courts 3/5/6 if either, you have booked the court, there is no booking for the current timeslot, or at least 2 of the booked players are not on court by 5 mins after the booking start time

Register for online booking

  1. Choose "Book a Court" menu option.
  2. Go to the 'New Registration' section
  3. Supply your Name, Email, and phone number
  4. Use club ID lccrathmines
  5. Your password will be emailed to you
  6. After first logging in, you can change your password via the 'Change Profile' link

Making a booking

  1. Choose "Book a Court" menu option.
  2. Select the date of your booking
  3. Select the start time of your booking
  4. Select booking duration - the usual 1.5 hour time restriction for doubles and 1 hour for singles applies. All singles matches in LCC club championships and Box Leagues can also book for 1.5 hours.
  5. Select 'Submit' button.
  6. Your booking should appear immediately on the online court schedule
  7. You should also receive an email confirmation of your booking.

Cancelling a booking

If you cannot fulfill a booking, always cancel in advance. Please try to cancel at least 24 hours in advance to give other players a chance to book that slot.

  1. Choose "Book a Court" menu option.
  2. Select the date of your booking
  3. Select the start time of your booking
  4. Select 'DELETE this reservation' button.
  5. Your booking should no longer appear on the online court schedule.
  6. You should also receive an email confirmation of your booking cancellation.
